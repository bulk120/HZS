About HZSAA

Howrah Zilla School Alumni Association is the officially registered organisation under Society Act of Govt of West Bengal (S/1L/63997) - founded on 1-Dec-2009.

Howrah Zilla School is one of the most premier educational institutions in the history of West Bengal having a legacy of 181+ years of producing unparallel stalwarts in every walk of life.

The Howrah Zilla School Alumni Association thrives to unite all the ex-students of Howrah Zilla School and build a strong community that would support not only the present students' community but also the broader society as a whole.

Our Activities

Health Camps

Organizing regular health camps to provide medical checkups and healthcare services to the community.

Special Classes

Conducting special classes and mentorship programs for current students to excel academically.

Social Initiatives

Engaging in various social welfare activities to support the broader community and society.
